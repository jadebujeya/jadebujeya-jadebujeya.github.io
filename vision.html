Our Vision and Our Mission

Mouse over the tree to learn about the seven elements of our mission.

  • "Form an active partnership with the community and the young people within it."
  • "Help provide real world insight on the issues young people face."
  • "Create a safe, fun and engaging way to engage young people."
  • "Build meaningful contributions to representation, research, advocacy and advisory committees on youth related issues."
  • "Make safe and engaging spaces for young people to have a voice in local government decisions, as well as the broader community."
  • "Create a society where young people are valued as the core ingredient of the future they are, and feel a sense of connection and belonging to their society in return."
  • "Bring empowerment to groups that are often misrepresented, misunderstood, marginalised, or are not able to participate."
